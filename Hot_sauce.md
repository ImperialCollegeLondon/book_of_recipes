
# Hot sauce
Hot sauce is a type of condiment, seasoning, or salsa made from chili peppers and other ingredients.

## Ingridients 
* 1 tomoto
* 2 chilli peppers
* 1 corriander
* 1 onion

## Instructions
* chop onions
* chop tomoto
* make a paste of them all together
* This is an imperfect receipe 
* add chilli peppers (diced) 
* add corriander
