# Garlic

Garlic sauce is a sauce prepared using garlic as a primary ingredient. More information [here](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Garlic_sauce).

## Ingredients

- 3 garlic cloves
- 150g Greek yogurt
- 1 lemon, juiced
- 2 tbsp tahini
- handful of chopped mint

## Instructions

- crush the garlic cloves
- combine everything
